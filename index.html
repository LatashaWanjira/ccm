About Us

What is CCM?

Câmara de Comércio de Moçambique, CCM is an association of non-profit national businesses and businesses incorporated under Mozambican law, that are autonomous in terms of their administration, finances and assets. CCM is dedicated to promoting the harmonious development of its members’ businesses, with a view to ensuring their effective participation in the country’s economic activities.

CCM's Mission

CCM’s core mission is to improve the competitiveness of its members and to help them to be active participants in international trade on a global scale. To enable this, CCM is charged with the responsibility of issuing a certificate of origin for each product that is exported out of Mozambique. The Certificate of Origin provides proof that the product comes from Mozambique and acts as a crucial marketing tool from Mozambique as a source of such and other products.
